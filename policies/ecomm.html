Electronic Communication

Use of Lists

OCI does not rent, trade, or sell email addresses. OCI may use email addresses that it has obtained during business interactions. OCI uses this information to contact customers, so that we may answer customer questions, acknowledge the receipt and dispatch of customer orders, send customer renewal notices, forward software problem notifications, and to send occasional announcements about OCI events, to those customers who have not opted out of receiving such announcements. These announcements are short straightforward messages that contain pointers to online resources where customers may explore the information more fully. Such announcements will be relevant to the products or services purchased.

Postal Mail

OCI may use the postal addresses of its customers to mail invoices, products, and announcements from OCI. The names and mailing addresses of customers who have not opted out (see below) may be used internally for rare special mailings. OCI customer names and mailing addresses are never sold or given to outside organizations or companies.

Opt-Out

Our site gives you the opportunity to opt out from either receiving email communications, or from having your name and address being used for future OCI Company activities. However to do so may result in your being unaware of any issues which might arise regarding software purchased through OCI. Opting out during the course of business transactions may result in our being unable to provide you with a quality experience.

To opt out send a blank email to optout (AT) objectcomputing.com.

If your job description changes and it is no longer appropriate to receive notifications please consider sending OCI a designee so as to maintain continuity.

Links

This site contains links to other sites in order to provide a more complete service. OCI is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of other such Web sites. Please use caution with your personal information if you are being redirected.

Contacting the Web Site

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, please contact OCI.
